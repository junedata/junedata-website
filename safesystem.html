What SafeSystem Does

SafeSystem is a powerful, API-first platform that revolutionizes how organizations track, analyze, and respond to traffic incidents. Built specifically for advocacy groups, research institutions, legal professionals, and government agencies, SafeSystem provides comprehensive oversight of the entire traffic safety ecosystemâ€”from initial collision data through judicial outcomes.

Key Capabilities

Intelligent Collision Tracking

Centralized incident database with precise geographic mapping and real-time data collection. Vulnerable Road User (VRU) identification for pedestrian and cyclist safety prioritization. Multi-jurisdiction coordination across police, EMS, and state reporting systems. Automated duplicate detection to ensure data integrity and prevent redundancy.

Evidence & Records Automation

Automated public records requests to government agencies for surveillance videos, crash reports, and documentation. Multi-platform credential management for seamless access to public records systems. Request status monitoring with real-time updates on approvals, denials, and releases. Intelligent evidence correlation linking videos, reports, and photos to specific incidents.

Judicial System Integration

Complete court proceedings tracking from citation through final disposition. Financial penalty monitoring including fines owed, paid, and outstanding balances. Warrant tracking for failure-to-appear cases. Judge and court performance analytics across multiple jurisdictions.

Legal Statute Reference

Comprehensive law database covering municipal ordinances, state statutes, and federal regulations. Violation tracking with penalty ranges and driver's license point assignments. Legal citation automation for accurate documentation and reporting.

Enterprise User Management

Role-based access control with geographic scope authorization (planet, state, county, municipality). Service account integration for automated data collection and API access. Audit trails for all user activities and data modifications. Secure credential storage for public records platform access.
